Instructions

Globus Transfer Hub assist configure high-speed data transfers

Quick Start:

  • 1. Log in with your organizational credentials or GlobusID. If you don't have an account yet, easily sign up and create a new Globus account
  • 2. Select a source node
  • 3. Browse through available datasets at the selected source node
  • 4. Choose destination node
  • 5. Confirm and initiate transfer
