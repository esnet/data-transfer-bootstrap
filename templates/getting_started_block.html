Instructions

Globus Transfer Hub assist configure high-speed data transfers

Quick Start:

  • 1. Log in: Use your organizational credentials or GlobusID. If needed, create a Globus account.
  • 2. Select Source: Choose a dataset collection and proceed to Globus site.
  • 3. Browse Datasets: Explore available datasets at the selected source.
  • 4. Confirm and Transfer: Review the transfer queue and start the transfer.
